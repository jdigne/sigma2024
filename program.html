Program

The meeting is organized around 16 invited one hour talks in the different subjects of expertise of SIGMA. These talks will be accessible to a large audience, and given by international experts. About half of them will discuss new connections with learning theory and data sciences. Furthermore, the meeting will also include 35 minutes contributed talks as well as poster sessions, which will in particular encourage young researchers to present their results.

If you want to attend, submit a talk proposal or a poster proposal (or both), see the registration/submission page.
