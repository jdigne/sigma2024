SIGMA 2024 Workshop

The meeting SIGMA’2024 is organized by SIGMA (Signal - Image - Geometry - Modelling - Approximation), one of the four activity groups of the SMAI (Société de Mathématiques Appliquées et Industrielles). This activity group aims at developing interactions between different mathematical communities, by overcoming somewhat artificial frontiers, and thus to create within SMAI new activities and synergies around topics such as signal and image processing, approximation theory, computational geometry and geometric modelling.
